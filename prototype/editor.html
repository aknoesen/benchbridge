SCH-11 interaction prototype  
place: click a part → it follows the cursor (R rotate, F flip) → click to drop  ·  hover + key: R rotate · F flip · D duplicate · E edit · X/Del delete  ·  select: click, or drag a box (Shift adds)  ·  wire: click a pin → move → click another pin (Esc cancels)  ·  right-click: menu
